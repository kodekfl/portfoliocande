ABOUT ME

I'm a multimedia creator driven by design—whether it’s crafting 3D pieces, editing videos, animating visuals, or experimenting with sound and interactive media. With a degree in Image and Sound Design, I’ve had the opportunity to contribute to diverse projects, including an advertising brand campaign, videography for live events, and content creation for vloggers.

Known for my reliability and ability to meet deadlines, I’m always striving to refine my skills in design, video, and sound production. Outside of work, I’m an avid explorer, continuously seeking new stories, cultures, and knowledge. For me, creativity is more than a profession; it’s a way to connect, experiment, and grow.
